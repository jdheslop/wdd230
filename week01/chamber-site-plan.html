Site Name

Freiberg am Neckar - Chamber of Commerce. This is a chamber of commerce website for the city of Freiberg am Neckar, Germany. It is one of the assignments for the WDD 230 - Web Frontend Development class.

Site Purpose

The Chamber of Commerce in Freiberg am Neckar provides information to local businesses and the community. It provides services, community information, news, and business spotlights.

Scenarios

1. What services does the Freiberg am Neckar Chamber of Commerce Offer?

2. What news or information is important for local businesses and the community?

3. What are examples of successful businesses and what can I learn from them?
